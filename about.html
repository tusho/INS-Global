WE LOVE NATURAL BEAUTY...

Beauty isn’t just about a face.
It’s about your mind. Your heart. And your soul.

We are great believers in embracing our natural beauty, and accentuating the gifts that we already bare. By giving skin and body the food it needs, we act as the catalysts to our own self-evolution. And with a healthy body, mind, heart and soul – we are beautiful.

We have been distributing since 2006, starting with the original Lucas Papaw Ointment manufactured in Australia. Since then we have expanded to produce our own natural skin products, as well as continue to grow a portfolio of products that we advocate and distribute internationally. Our ambition is to work with other brands with similar philosophies so that we may grow together.

With our global network and experience in distributing brands and products internationally, we can help you by supporting you with your marketing, advertising, social media, packaging, pricing and point of sale.
